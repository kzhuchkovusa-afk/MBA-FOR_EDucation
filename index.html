Frequently Asked Questions

Is this for public school teachers? ▼

No. This is specifically for K-12 private school owners and tutoring center operators who run a business. Public school teachers won't find this useful—it's focused on business operations, marketing, and enrollment.

What if I'm just starting my school? ▼

If you're pre-revenue or under $50K/year, this might be too advanced. The training assumes you already have students and are working on scaling operations. Focus on getting your first 10-20 students first, then come back.

How much time does this require per week? ▼

Each lesson is 5-15 minutes. You can complete one module per week in under an hour. The focus is implementation, not consuming hours of content. Most members spend 2-4 hours per week between learning and implementing.

Can I cancel anytime? ▼

Yes. Cancel anytime, no penalties. If you cancel within the first 30 days, you'll get a 100% refund, no questions asked.

Do you offer one-on-one consulting? ▼

Not directly through this membership. However, we host monthly live Q&A sessions where you can ask specific questions. If you need dedicated 1-on-1 support, reach out to info@gocoding.tech and we'll discuss custom options.

What if I have a question that's not covered in the courses? ▼

You can ask in the private community or bring it to the monthly live Q&A sessions. The community is active, and most questions get answered within a few hours.
